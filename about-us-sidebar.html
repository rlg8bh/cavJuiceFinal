Our Companies Mission Statement

Cav Juice is cold-pressed juice weekly delivery service, committed to providing healthy, convenient products at a reasonable price. Our juice is produced in-house, made with the freshest ingredients and used for our publicly acclaimed juice combinations. Although Cav Juice was founded in 2018, the founding team has been experimenting with juices since 2008. What began as gift from a friend, founders Rachel Goode, Ian Davis, and Hälsing Nordin have turned juicing into a passion. Word of the team’s magic touch eventually got around UVa’s Grounds, and soon every student wanted their juice. Wanting to provide these college students with their tasty, nutritional drinks, they decided they wanted to commercialize their product. However, being students, they knew that Charlottesville lacked cheaper and more convenient options than the current smoothie and juice providers. In effect, the team revolutionized juice consumption by proposing weekly juice deliveries, and Cav Juice was born. Since, students have praised the introduction of convenient, cheaper health into their college lives.
